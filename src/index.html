About Us

Long Reach Marlins (officially Long Reach Marlins, Inc.) is a community swim team based in Columbia, Maryland. We operate as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit to support youth swimming, fitness, and community involvement. Our volunteers coach, time, and organize activities to make swimming accessible and fun.

Programs

We offer seasonal swim teams, learn-to-swim support, and community swim events. Check back for program details for the 2026 swim season!

Join / Membership

Membership is open to swimmers of all ages and abilities. To express interest, email us at admin@longreachmarlins.org.
